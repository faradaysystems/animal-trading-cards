Hedgehog

"Hedgehogs are largely immune to snake venom,they possess between 5,000-7,000.

Bonus fact: like in computer science, a group of hedgehogs is called an array!"

  • Scientific Name: Erinaceinae
  • Average Length and Mass: 25 cm, 800 gr.
  • Average Lifespan: 5 years
  • Habitat: Habitat Generalists

During hibernation, the body temperature of a hedgehog can decrease to about 2 °C (36 °F).

When the animal awakes from hibernation, the body temperature rises from 2–5 °C (36–41 °F) back to its normal 30–35 °C (86–95 °F) body temperature.
